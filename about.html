About Us

Founded nearly 8 years ago and guided by the simple, yet profound, philosophy of ‘Delivering more than expected’, Shrushti Abhilasha Infra is a leading real estate developer In Maval & Mulshi with maintaining a dominant presence in Punes residential market.

Vision

At Shrushti Abhilasha Infra, we are a leading real estate developer committed to creating exceptional living environments that stand the test of time. Our Vision is to transform the way people experience living spaces, providing them with a sanctuary that goes beyond mere shelter.

We believe in the power of design to shape lives, and our Vision is rooted in creating spaces that harmonize with nature, encourage human connection, and promote a sense of belonging. We envision neighborhoods where residents can thrive, fostering a strong sense of community and shared values.

Mission

Our operating philosophy is team work with our customers. With that in mind, we strive to be a performer and leader in every dimension of our business. We never substitute price for quality because our goal is 100% customer satisfaction, and ultimately, long-term success for the business.

Values

Accountability | Commitment to delivery schedules | Team work | Customer centricity | Transparency and long term partnership with stakeholders | Innovation in design, systems and world class processes | Superior quality minus price-induced compromise
